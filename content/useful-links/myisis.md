# [myISIS (Integrated Student Information System)](https://myisis.nus.edu.sg/)
Students are required to pay their school fees here.

Personal particulars are to be updated here as well.

Unofficial transcripts for up till the current semester is available to download.

Exam schedules and seating plans will be available here.

S/U declaration is to be done here after results are released.
